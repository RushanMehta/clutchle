How To Play

Welcome to Clutchle! This is a high-stakes twist on traditional word games.

The Objective:

You must find the secret 5-letter word on the 6th (final) attempt. If you solve it early, or fail to find it on the last try, you lose!

The Rules:

  • Guesses 1–5: Use these rounds as "intelligence gathering" to discover green and yellow letter clues. Try to find letters without accidentally guessing the exact secret word!
  • Guess 6: You must clutch the win by submitting the correct secret word.
